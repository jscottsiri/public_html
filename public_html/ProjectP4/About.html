The Story of Myself

Hello, here is some basic information about me for the time being: I grew up in a town in Northern New Jersey called Pequannock and attended Pequannock Township High School.


During my time at PTHS, I earned several accomplishments. These included earning my Eagle Scout with Troop 79 and attending Boys State and earning the rank of Assemblyman. I was accepted to the New Jersey Institute of Technology as a conditional honors student. After doing well my first semester, I became a full honors student. I am currently a sophomore and intend to complete my degree at NJIT.
